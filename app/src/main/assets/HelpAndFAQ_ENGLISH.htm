Introduction

What is consumer application?

Consumer Client Application (Android Version) is a mobile application that works on all smart phones that support Android 2.0 OS. This application helps the consumers to manage financial/non-financial services.

 

On which handsets does consumer application work?

Consumer Client Application (Android Version) can be installed in all smart phones that support Android 2.0 OS

Application on their mobile and access the financial/non financial services.

 

Do I need to have a connection from a specific mobile service operator?

No, the specific mobile service operator is not required; it works across almost all operators in India. Check the web site for more details.

 

Do I need a GPRS plan for the application?

No, you need not have a GPRS plan to use the application. The application uses SMS for which the standard rates as per your operator will apply.

 

What if I lose my mobile phone?

Your consumer application account will remain secure even though you lose the mobile phone, since your application password and transaction MPIN are known only to you.

 

How do I download the consumer app?

You can visit the Google Play store depending on your phone and search for consumer client application by using the keyword consumer app. You can click on install and download the app.

How do I access my app after downloading it from the app store?

You will need an active internet service (2G / 3G / 4G / Wi-Fi) to use consumer application.

What are the features on the Consumer Client mobile app?

The following services will be available on Consumer Client mobile app are:

      Send money

      Top Up

      Bill payment

      Merchant payment

      Balance Inquiry

      View your statements

      Cash deposit

      Cash out

         Personalize your Consumer App

Do I need a minimum eligibility to register for the services?

If you are an Indian national having a mobile number and above the age of 18 Years, then you are eligible to download and use the consumer application services.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Activation

How to activate consumer application in my mobile and create my account?

1.     Start the consumer application installed on your mobile phone.

2.     The customer registers to the services

3.     Customer has to provide the following information an upload required identification

Documents for verification and processing.

         First Name

         Last Name

         Mobile Number

         Date of Birth

         Gender

         ID Type

         ID Number

4.     Click Next.

On successful validation, you will receive a message as your application has been registered successfully.

5.     The customers registered are supposed to activate the account.

6.     On main login screen click on activate & provide the following information:

         Mobile Number

         Set MPIN

         Confirmation of New PIN

         Mothers Birth Date (the answer to your mothers date of birth query is kept as a secret answer which will be used as your identification for all future references)

         Customer Date of Birth

7.     Click Next.

8.     A screen next appears with Issuers Terms and Conditions

9.     Review the terms and conditions and press Confirm.

On successful validation, you will receive a message as your account has been successfully activated.

You are now ready to use Consumer Application.

 

The Consumer Application tried registering my mobile number. However, my number is already registered by someone else in the system? Can I still register?

You can still register. Your registration will be queued and a back office team will investigate the matter. You will be informed whether your account was successfully registered or declined. In case your registration was declined, you can still register with a different mobile number.

 

What happens if my mobile number is re-issued to someone else?

You must always inform customer care of your change in mobile number so that your account can be delinked from your old number and linked to your new one. In case you forget to do so, and your old mobile number is reissued to somebody else, the new customer will not be able to access your account since he/she does not have your MPIN.

 

What do I do if I buy a new phone?

Your consumer application account will remain secure even though you change the mobile phone, since your application password and transaction MPIN are known only to you. For the latest information on handsets that support the application, visit our website.

 

Am I allowed to register for more than one account?

Yes. You are allowed to register for more than one account.

 

How do I activate my account?

To activate your account, Click ACTIVATE tab on the main login screen. The application process you to provide the necessary information & set new MPIN an confirm the issuers terms and conditions. In response, you will receive a message as your account has been successfully activated.

 

Do I need to activate my account again if I have already activated using the Consumer Application on my phone?

No, you need not activate your account again if it is already activated through Consumer Application. The process remains the same irrespective.

I downloaded the app and registered successfully, but I could not login to the app and was given an error.

Please re-try logging in after some time; it happens due to technical error or net work connection sometimes. You can login with the same user Mobile Number & MPIN which you created by tapping on the Activate in the app.

Cash Deposits

How to deposit money into my prepaid account?

Once you are registered to consumer application and have activated your account, you need to load money into your new prepaid account. Currently, you can deposit cash by visiting an authorized Consumer Application Agent location.

To find an authorized Agent and deposit cash:

1.     Visit our web site customercare@yts.net.in to know more about Agent locations or call customer helpline.

2.     Visit an authorized consumer application Agent along with an ID.

3.     Give cash to the Agent and show ID proof. The Agent will credit the money to your account after confirming your identity.

4.     You can start making payments instantly using your mobile. You will get an SMS with your new account balance and a transaction ID.

In case of any disputes call Customer Helpline on 1800 3000 4300.

 

When I went to deposit cash with an Agent, the Agent took my cash, but I didnt get any SMS confirming the deposit.

There may be network or technical error delays which could affect the response time. Please check your activity history or account balance. If that is also unresponsive due to network delays, wait for some time and then call customer Helpline number 1800 3000 4300.from the store. Please ensure that you get the confirmation through any one of the options mentioned above, before you leave Agent location.

Here you can send money to a registered customer or not registered customer of the consumer application by providing their mobile number, amount an optional message. You and the recipient customer will be notified on successful completion of the transaction.

o    Amount: Enter the amount you wish to send.

         The application will now prompt to authorize the transaction by providing the MPIN

After a successful validation you will receive message on success or failure of transaction initiated.

What payment methods can I choose to Send Money?

The payment modes available in the consumer application to send money are as follows

         Net Banking

         Credit Card

         Debit Card

How do I select the recipient to Send Money?

There are 2 types of recipients, i.e. Registered recipient (p2p) and Non-registered recipient (vp2p):

 

Registered Recipient (p2p): Customers who are already having a Consumer Application wallet are known as registered customers. You can select their name from Favorites or just type their name to select.

Non-registered Recipient (vp2p): The Send Money to Non-registered Recipient facility enables you to transfer funds to contacts that are not registered for consumer Application. For non-registered recipient, the mobile number can be entered directly to transfer the funds.

Will the non-registered recipient need to enter a MPIN / Secret Code to retrieve the money?

Yes, a Secret Code will be sent to you, once you have initiated a Send Money request. You will have to share the Secret Code separately with the recipient to enable him/her to retrieve the amount.

Can the recipient reject the payment sent to him?

A recipient can either reject the payment sent or allow the pending payment request.

What happens if the recipient does not take any action?

In case the recipient does not claim the money in 5 days by registering for a Wallet, then the amount will be credited back to your Wallet and a notification about the reversal will be sent to you.

I don't have bank account details of my friends to whom I want to send money. Can I still send money using Consumer Client Application?

You can still send money to your friend using Send Money feature on Consumer Client Application. Using this facility you can send money via mobile phone contact number.

 

Bill Payments

Can I get the list of billers available through Consumer Application?

Yes, you will receive the list of available billers in consumer application.

 

How many billers can I add?

There is no limit to the number of billers you can add from the list of participating billers.

 

How do I add a biller using the Consumer Application?

You have to initially search for a biller through mobile application using biller code or biller category, name and location. Once the biller details are retrieved, you can provide information such as nickname for the biller and reference details specific to each biller. Once you are done, you will receive a notification as biller added. You can find the biller details in the View biller option in Consumer Application or whenever you initiate the transaction for any billers at the end of the transactions shows a notification as add biller to list. You can directly add the biller to your list.

 

How do I make bill payments through Consumer Application?

How you pay depends on the biller you wish to pay. Some billers can be paid only when they raise a bill, whereas some billers can be paid at any time.

 

Do I have to pay the entire bill amount?

You can make a payment either in full or in part depending upon whether the biller provides you with that facility. For instance, currently, you can pay your Vodafone bill in part, but you need to pay your MTNL bill in full.

 

When does the biller receive my payment?

The biller will receive the payment within a few days of your making it through the application.

 

By when should I make my bill payments?

To ensure that your payments arrive before the bills due date and to avoid late payment charges, you need to make your payment at least 4 working days before the bills due date. Consumer Application cannot accept payments after the due date.

 

Do I have an option to delete a registered biller using the Consumer Application?

No, you dont have an option to delete a registered biller in the Consumer application.

 

 

Do I have an option to edit a registered biller using the Consumer Application?

No, you dont have an option to edit a registered biller in the Consumer application.

 

Can I add/edit/delete a Biller by calling Customer Helpline if my handset application is not working?

Yes, you can call Customer Helpline to update your biller information. The Customer Helpline executive will authenticate you before taking your request.

 

 

Top Up/ Prepared Recharge

What is prepaid mobile recharge?

Prepaid mobile recharge is a facility to recharge your prepaid mobile connection using any of the available channels supported by Consumer Application. You can also recharge other prepaid mobile numbers as well using this feature. You need to provide the prepaid mobile number and the amount to recharge.

 

How do I recharge my prepaid mobile connection?

You should be a registered customer of consumer application and should have the consumer application downloaded on your android mobile phone. Note: (only prepaid mobile connections can be recharged).

 

Do I get a confirmation when I initiate a recharge?

Yes, you will be sent a confirmation SMS once the recharge is successful. In case you are recharging a different mobile number, a confirmation message will also be sent to that respective mobile number. If the mobile number you provided for recharging is not valid, you will receive an SMS message as failure of recharge with the error code.

 

Are all operators supported?

Yes, most of the operators are supported. If a particular operator is not supported, you will receive an appropriate message.

 

Can I recharge my mobile for any denomination?

No, you cannot recharge for any denomination. You can only recharge for denominations permitted by your operator. Before you specify the recharge amount check whether the amount is permitted by your operator. Please refer the User Guide or Website for the minimum and maximum amounts that can be recharged using our service. (The denominations under consumer application are MOBILE & DTH top.)

 

I am unable to recharge for a specific amount even though that denomination is permitted by my operator?

You may be recharging for a valid denomination, but make sure you have sufficient funds in your account before recharging. In case you have insufficient funds you can recharge for a lower permitted denomination.

Why is my prepaid mobile recharge request rejected?

Your prepaid mobile wallet recharge request may be rejected due to any one of the following reasons:

         Invalid mobile number

         Using a postpaid mobile number

         Entering wrong amount for recharge

         Transaction request exceeds daily/monthly amount

Can I recharge someone elses mobile number?

Yes, you can recharge someone elses mobile number. The other person need not have consumer application account. Note: (Recharge can be done only for the prepared mobile numbers)

 

Is there any additional fee charged for prepaid mobile recharge?

There may be a fee charged for prepaid mobile recharge. Please lookup our Fee Schedule for up-to-date information.

 

What happens if I enter an incorrect MPIN?

In case you enter an incorrect MPIN you will receive a notification message as saying invalid MPIN with error code & been requested to re-enter the MPIN. However if three consecutive incorrect MPINs are entered your account gets locked.

Do I need to have a bank account to pay my bills?

No, you can pay your bills using the wallet balance.

Change MPIN

What is Change MPIN?

Change MPIN is a feature in the application that allows you to change your current MPIN on-demand. You can change your MPIN by providing your current MPIN and then setting a new MPIN. After validating that the information is correct, your MPIN will be changed and you will be notified with a successful message.

 

Can I change my MPIN even if I remember my old MPIN?
Yes, you can change your 4 digit MPIN number in spite of knowing your current MPIN for security reasons. You need to specify a new 4 digit MPIN and also enter you current MPIN.

 

Are there any charges when I change my MPIN?

No, its a non financial transaction since you will not be charged any fees for changing your MPIN.

 

How often can I change my MPIN?

You can change your MPIN en number of time or whenever you need for security purpose. There are no restrictions on the number of times you change your MPIN.

 

Will I be forced to change my MPIN at regular intervals?

No, you will not be forced to change your MPIN. However it is advised that you change your MPIN at regular intervals for security reasons.

 

What are the MPIN rules that should be fulfilled when the MPIN is setup?

MPIN should be numeric and should be 4 digits in length. There should be no spaces between the digits. The new MPIN must not be same as the current MPIN.

 

Can you recommend any security guidelines?

         Ensure that your MPIN is unique and not easily guessed.

         Never share your MPIN with anyone.

         Do not store your MPIN or other sensitive information on your mobile phone.

         Do not set repetitive or consecutive 4 digits e.g. 1111 or 1234.

      Do not set your date of birth as your MPIN.

3.     Select Change MPIN from the option.

4.     Enter your current password.

5.     Enter the new password.

6.     Re-enter your new password to confirm

7.     Click confirm

After a successful verification you will receive message that your MPIN has been changed.

Is there any rule for setting a Password?

Yes, your password should be 4 alphanumeric characters & the new password entering should not be similar as old password.
